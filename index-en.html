About Us

"La casa de Qori" welcomes you to Ica, the sunny, sweet and generous soil.

This familiar guesthouse is known for providing guests a pleasant environment conducive to rest and leisure. The host is the family García-Hesse who want to make their guests feel as comfortable as at home.

Among our offered services: Accommodation with breakfast, tourist information, guide to a bodega.

Our mission: to once again emphasize the importance of tourism for the Department of Ica and its provinces.

How to arrive

The guesthouse is located in the district Subtanjalla province of Ica, at the height of 297 km of the Panamericana Sur. From Lima, you can reach us by car or bus such as Cruz del Sur (touristic 3,5h) or Soyuz (economic 4,5 h).
